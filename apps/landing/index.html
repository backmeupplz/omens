Omens monitors Twitter, Reddit, and more — then uses AI to surface only what matters to you.

Open Source

How it works

1

Connect sources

Add subreddits, Twitter accounts, or RSS feeds you want to monitor.

2

Describe your interests

Tell the AI what matters to you in plain language.

3

Get signal

Omens polls your sources, scores each item 0-100, and surfaces only what's relevant.

CLI

$ omens config --api-url https://app.omens.online --api-key omens_abc123_...
$ omens sources add --type reddit --config '{"subreddits":["technology","programming"]}'
$ omens feed --min-score 60
